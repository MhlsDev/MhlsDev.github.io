Codecademy CLL moderator and Best Wishes for Christmas holiday season

Thursday, December 6, 2018


Well, its the time of the year where Christmas day is almost here and the new year is about to come knocking at our door once again. This Christmas has been a bit special for me, as i have been asked to, and yes, got to become a moderator in Codecademy's Lifelong Learners online community. For those of you that are not familiar with Codecademy, its an online school for those that want to learn coding.

More

It is an awesome opportunity for me, to become an active part of Codecademy's community at this point in my career, mostly because i get to participate in projects that will advance my coding skills rapidly. The school offers plenty of courses and intensives for lots of coding languages like JavaScript, Python, Java, Ruby, C++, and of course HTML, CSS etc. but also paths that will guide you to specific skill sets. Lot’s of the material is free, but paid pro plans and intensives are offered if you really want to get serious about learning.


Personally in January I begin my JavaScript intensive, so i can extend my JS skills even more. JS is not the easiest programming language to learn, mostly because of it’s many quirks, but with the help of CLL’s online community, one has nothing to fear. Coding is an important skill for the future, whether you are a tech geek like me or not. The future is online, and being able to understand even a small piece of this amazing world, is essential imho.


So, i wanna wish you all a happy holidays and a happy new year. May the new year fulfill our dreams, hopes, needs, help us evolve as human beings, and become better persons in our everyday life.
