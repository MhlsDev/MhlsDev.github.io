More

I guess at this moment, that's pretty much the need i am trying to fulfil.


Now i am kind of anxious to upload my new blog section to the live website, so this post is going to be kind of short. Hopefully in the coming months, i will be able to share much more with you, during my design/developer/coding journeys. Expect this website to evolve, change, shrink or grow, but it will always maintain it’s minimalistic approach, since that is basically my personal design philosophy.
